Before You Submit

Please read the full Call for Papers carefully. In summary:

  • Format: Extended abstract or position paper, 2–4 pages in IEEE conference format (excluding references)
  • Supplementary material: You may optionally include a supplementary video or figures illustrating geometric structure, contact-rich interaction, or representation-driven behaviour
  • Review: All submissions are peer-reviewed by at least two domain experts
  • Acceptance: Accepted papers will be presented as spotlight teaser talks and interactive posters at the workshop
