Session Formats

  • Keynote Talks: 30-minute presentations by invited speakers, each followed by 5 minutes of Q&A.
  • Thematic Panel Discussions: 15-minute focused discussions following each keynote block, with all block speakers and audience participation.
  • Spotlight Teaser Talks: 3–5 minute presentations by authors of accepted contributed papers, encouraging discussion at the poster session.
  • Poster / Demo Sessions: Interactive sessions during breaks where contributors present their work and receive direct feedback from speakers and attendees.
  • Interdisciplinary Impact Clinics (IIC): Small-group mentoring sessions where early-career researchers receive tailored feedback from senior invited speakers on their research approaches and directions.
